Welcome to Campus Foodie

Hello! I'm your campus foodie, here to share the best eats around campus. Navigating a busy campus and having many food options can be overwhelming, but don't worry. All of the recommended spots are just a few clicks away from the Petty building. Centrally located, these eateries are perfect for a quick bite between classes or a leisurely meal with friends. From hidden gems to popular spots, I've got you covered!

   
       

Featured Eats

       
                   

Sushi Republic

                                       

Located on Tate Street, Sushi Republic is an award-winning sushi restaurant located next to the UNCG campus. Serving both classic Japanese sushi and diverse fusion dishes inspired by different cultures. Dine on creative maki rolls, bento boxes, and noodles.

                   

Crazy Mexico

                                       

Crazy Mexico #4 has officially opened its doors at 332 Tate St., marking its first location in Greensboro. Known for its vibrant atmosphere, flavorful dishes, and refreshing margaritas, this restaurant is set to become a go-to destination for UNCG students and locals alike. Whether you’re in the mood for sizzling fajitas, cheesy enchiladas, or their signature Crazy Tacos, there’s something for everyone on the menu. Plus, with its convenient location near campus, it’s the perfect place to grab a bite with friends or enjoy a casual night out.

                   

Tate Street Coffee House

                                       

Waking up Greensboro since 1993. Conveniently located near the University of North Carolina at Greensboro, Tate Street Coffee House is the perfect spot to enjoy a warm cup of coffee to wake you up in the morning, keep you going through the afternoon or power you well into the night. Open from 6:30am to 5:00pm daily, our cozy spot will be your new favorite relaxing retreat. We serve breakfast favorites like bagels, muffins and pastries, hearty lunchtime options and decadent desserts.
